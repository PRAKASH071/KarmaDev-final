Case study

Jai Ho Property to Prosperity

Our website aims at the client “ Jai Ho Infrastructures” dedicated to their craft.They strive to surpass clients’ expectations by following a Through Property consultancy and design process and maintaining a high standard of construction quality control with fully licensed & insured professionals.Property Consultancy

overview

Why did the client choose KarmaDev.in?

  • Flexibility and Convenience

  • Cost-Effective

  • Personalized user Experience

  • Community of Learners

Project Process

Our Involvement

  • UI /Ux Design

  • Complete development process

  • Testing

  • Website Maintenance

Requirements

The project was preceded by a team meeting to discuss all of the client requirements. This extensive analysis simplifies, accelerates, and lowers the cost of delivery.

This meeting was led by our Project Manager, who assists the team throughout the project, and our Developer, who ensures all technical details are covered.

The client provided a detailed brief outlining the technological requirements and limitations, the vision for the website, and the existing infrastructure. It was a difficult task with a short deadline



KarmaDev.in’s Role and Involvement in Jai Ho

In collaboration with Jai Ho, KarmaDev.in created a front-end interface for this landing environment.

It had to guarantee simple user navigation, information that was easily accessible to encourage trust, and effective front-end and back-end integration.

Result

During the Project

KarmaDev.in had to ensure that the interface worked flawlessly with the existing infrastructure and database. The aesthetics must also be consistent with the brand guidelines and the current website.

Jai Ho was able to offer this with just one developer in order to save money and speed up delivery.

KEY Findings

  • This website would clearly state the services offered by the company. This could include services such as general contracting, design-build, construction management, and other related services.

  • The website would showcase the company's past projects, highlighting the quality of work and range of projects completed.

  • This website would highlight the company's adherence to industry standards, such as LEED certification or other sustainability initiatives.

  • This website would display any licenses and accreditations held by the company, including state and local certifications and affiliations with industry organizations
